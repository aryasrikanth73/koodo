ENRICHMENT PROGRAMS

We Believe that learning should not be limited to a specific boundary and time schedule, but kids need to experience more than what is being taught in books.

Enrichment program enables students to realize their potential in a variety of settings beyond the classroom.

Through educational enrichment programs, students can develop a sense of self-awareness and understand their schoolwork better with enrichment programs that are in line with our school curriculum.

They also learn how to positively grow their interests and skills to have a more robust understanding of their potential.

Our services

Enrichment Programs

ART

In this workshops, young artists will be introduced to mediums and techniques for creative expression. This program will act as a springboard for children to explore, experiment and create their own authentic art pieces. Children will continue to develop their skills of observing.

CHESS

We will teach basics of playing chess, making it fun every step of the way. Chess is a wonderful game for boosting pattern-recognition and strategic-thinking skills, as well as learning about good sportsmanship

SIGN LANGUAGE

Sign Language teaches children to speak to those who cannot hear. Through the use of fingers and hand gestures, facial and torso movements. Students will learn the essentials of this important bridge language, through games and group activities.

DANCE

Leap, dash and sway! Children will develop their coordination, balance, flexibility and endurance through creative movement. By learning the basics of ballet and tap, children will be introduced to dance as an art form, one that brings together inspiring music.

MUSIC

Inspire children to enjoy all aspects of music as they jam to a variety of styles. While singing, dancing, chanting and playing instruments, children learn to joyfully express themselves, work with their peers, and understand and appreciate music.

SCIENCE LAB

Intriguing demonstrations, hands- on experiments and discussion. Whether children are bending light to see all the colors of the rainbow or building their very own model rockets, they will engage with science through activities that grow their understanding of how the world works.
