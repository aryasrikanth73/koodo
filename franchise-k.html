Franchise Requirements

Here are the basic requirements to start your own Koodo Pre-School:

Investment

Minimum initial investment of $50,000. This ensures you have the necessary financial foundation to start and grow your business.

Space

A facility with at least 2,500 square feet is required to accommodate the learning spaces and recreational areas for children.

Passion

A passion for education and working with young children is a must. Dedication to shaping the next generation is at the core of our mission.
